Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Built for You

Chaltus was founded on the belief that every client deserves more than a quick appointment — they deserve a stylist who truly understands their hair. We bring deep roots in African and Ethiopian hair care traditions to every single service.

Our signature Ethiopian Butter Deep Conditioning is just one example of how we blend traditional techniques with modern precision. From your first braid to your hundredth re-twist, we treat your hair like it matters — because it does.
